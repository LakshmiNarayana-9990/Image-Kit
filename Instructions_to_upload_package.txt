
1. To create the .tar and wheel files.

python setup.py sdist bdist_wheel

2. To check that the distribution is PASSED or not.

twine check dist/*

3. To test the package 
twine upload --repository-url https://test.pypi.org/legacy/ dist/*


4. Finally Upload the package.

twine upload dist/*
